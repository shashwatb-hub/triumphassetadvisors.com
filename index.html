Hyderabad Market Brief

Hyderabad's commercial real estate market enters Q1 2026 with structural momentum intact. HITEC City Grade-A office rents hold firm at ₹72 per sq ft per month against a sub-6% vacancy rate, driven by sustained GCC and IT/ITeS demand that absorbed 3.2 million sq ft in Q2 2025 — a 23% year-on-year increase. The Financial District is consolidating as the city's next premium nucleus, with rental values 25% above pre-pandemic levels. Retail corridor demand accelerates across Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, and Kondapur, driven by organised retail expansion. Rental yields in prime IT corridors sustain a 4-6% band with a 10-year CAGR of 11-12%. Emerging sub-markets of Kokapet and Narsingi offer 10-15% rental arbitrage versus HITEC City.
